Venues

Each venue has a different vibe and style.

You may prefer the gym facilities offered by a dance studio, or relaxed social atmosphere of a stylish gastro-pub, or hotel . Whatever your choice each venue offers total privacy and shares 3 and 6 super grippy Titanium X Poles used between 8 to 24 students.

For courses currently running at these venues see Timetable.
