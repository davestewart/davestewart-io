Subscribe

Subscribing to FunkyFitFirm News, or the Social Pole Club are two FREE ways that you can join the poledancing community by keeping your finger on the pulse for any new courses, or poledancing events.

You won’t miss out on opportunities to book courses at our new venues, or to contribute to exciting poledancing performances, charity showcases and World Record attempts. These events are great ways to meet, or keep in touch with the poledancing friends who keep your passion for pole fitness alive.

Subscribe to FunkyFitFirm News

You can subscribe to our monthly newsletter to keep you informed of new courses and venues giving you the first opportunity to book.

You will also be kept up to date with forthcoming poledance fitness community opportunities where you can develop your skills and connect with other poledancers.

Subscribe to Social Pole Club

You can join our Social Pole Club to receive advance notice and discounted entry to these events. These happen every one to three months.
