One to One

One to One Classes are for those who prefer a more intensive, bespoke, or private experience. This is often also a choice or those who want to specifically build personal confidence.

You choose technical pole coaching, pole fitness, confidence building, performance skills, or a combination of these for your tailored class. And like a personal training session you will be offered a program tailored to your specific needs to help you develop functional pole strength and flexibility between classes.

Venues

We can bring a pole to you (if your home is suitable), or use one of our venues if you prefer.

Please contact us for a bespoke price and see our venue list in Venues.
