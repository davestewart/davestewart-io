Hen Parties

A poledancing party is a great way to celebrate a hen party, birthday, or start off a killer night out with the girls.

After a fun warm up we will teach you some poledance moves and spins incorporating games to get you warm, relaxed and laughing!

The event can be as special as you want to make it with our team of professional prop and costume artists, beauty experts and party producers.

We can include burlesque, DJs, massage, make-up, prop and costume styling, catering, visuals and stylish reportage photography. You can even produce a DVD, so you can play back the hilarious highlights of your big night.

From £240 for up to 15 girls.
