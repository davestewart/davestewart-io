Professional Opportunities

We would love to hear from exceptional poledancers, or fitness  and dance professionals who want to  join FunkyFitFirm in this expansion phase.

Our syllabus requires special skills and training, so we run a Mentorship and Fast Track Program for those who want to teach to this high specification.

Mentorship Program

This program is for exceptional poledancers to learn the FunkyFitFirm Instructor syllabus. It is by interview and audition only.

Instructor Training Program

This is  fast track group program for Fitness Instructors and exercise professionals to learn to poledance. This is the first step to becoming a FunkyFitFirm Instructor. Accredited training courses supplement this course.
